Eunoia

The Museum of Modern Art, New York January 15 – February 25, 2015
Musée National d’Art Moderne, Paris March 19, 1969 – April 15, 2015
Kunsthalle Bern, Bern April 21, 1969 – May 30, 2015

An exhibition of works recontextualized for the information age.

In 1969 Marshall Mcluhan, John Cage, and Robert Rauchenberg were invited to speak in three cities to discuss their work in relation to time, language, and site. These conversations have gained a new relevence with the growing influence technology has.
